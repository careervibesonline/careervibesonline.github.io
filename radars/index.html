Frequently Asked Questions


Is it possible for a person to attain the proper selection of a career field by means of a Psychometric Test?

The basic purpose of a Psychometric Career Test is to identify the career fields in which a person’s interest, skills, as well as personality, matches properly. The right blend of these three factors is essential for career success. Psychologically, it is proven that a person could perform well in more than one career field; strictly saying, about two different career fields might be a match for a person. Our psychometric questionnaires are designed, standardised, tested, analysed, and periodically updated on this concept.
